DR. S.M. MOFIJUL ISLAM — PERSONAL ACADEMIC WEBSITE
===================================================

WHAT'S IN THIS FOLDER
- index.html      → your website in English
- index-bn.html   → your website in Bengali (বাংলা)
- cv/             → your CV as a PDF (linked by the "Download CV" button)
- images/         → put your photos here
- README.txt      → this guide

The two language pages are linked: an "English / বাংলা" button in the top
menu switches between them. Keep both files (and the cv and images folders)
together so the links and the CV download button all work.

To replace the CV later, just put your new PDF in the cv folder and name it
exactly:  SM-Mofijul-Islam-CV.pdf

--------------------------------------------------
STEP 1 — ADD YOUR PHOTOS
--------------------------------------------------
Put your image files in the "images" folder, named EXACTLY like this
(lowercase, .jpg). If a photo is missing, a neat labelled box shows in its
place, so the site never looks broken.

  images/portrait.jpg    professional headshot (shown big in the header)
  images/about.jpg       you in the field or lab
  images/award1.jpg      award / ceremony photo
  images/award2.jpg      award / ceremony photo
  images/award3.jpg      award / ceremony photo
  images/workshop1.jpg   workshop / conference
  images/workshop2.jpg   workshop / conference
  images/workshop3.jpg   wide conference photo (shows as a wide box)
  images/workshop4.jpg   fieldwork

Tip: keep each photo under ~1 MB so the site loads fast. Any free tool
(e.g. squoosh.app) can shrink a large photo.

--------------------------------------------------
STEP 2 — ADD A VIDEO (optional)
--------------------------------------------------
1. Upload your video to YouTube.
2. On the video, click Share → Embed → Copy the <iframe ...> code.
3. Open index.html in any text editor, search for "VIDEO SLOT",
   and paste the iframe where the comment tells you to.

--------------------------------------------------
STEP 3 — PUT IT ONLINE FOR FREE
--------------------------------------------------
EASIEST (recommended) — Netlify Drop:
  1. Go to  app.netlify.com/drop
  2. Drag this whole folder onto the page.
  3. Done — you get a free live link instantly. (Free account lets you
     rename it to something like  mofijul-islam.netlify.app)

ALSO FREE — GitHub Pages:
  1. Create a free github.com account.
  2. Make a repository named  yourusername.github.io
  3. Upload index.html and the images folder.
  4. Your site is live at  https://yourusername.github.io

ABOUT GOOGLE SITES:
  Google Sites cannot host this custom design directly — it only accepts
  content typed into its own editor and strips custom HTML/CSS, so the
  professional look would be lost. Netlify Drop or GitHub Pages are just
  as free and keep the design exactly as shown. If you still prefer Google
  Sites, ask and I'll prepare a version organised for its editor.
